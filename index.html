The scientific way to create a diet for your dog.

FITDOG is an easy-to-use tool providing food serving suggestions for your dog, which are customized to their specific needs.

This calculation follows the guidelines of academic studies and produces results based on mass rather than volume.

How to use this tool

Below are various RER values that represent dogs of different age and fitness levels.

Enter the number that best represents your dog to ensure an accurate calculation.

  • Neutered adult 1.6 x RER
  • Intact adult 1.8 x RER
  • Inactive/obese prone 1.2-1.4 x RER
  • Weight loss 1.0 x RER for ideal weight
  • Weight gain 1.2-1.8 x RER for ideal weight
  • Active, working dogs 2.0-5.0 x RER
  • Puppy 0-4 months 3.0 x RER
  • Puppy 4 months to adult 2.0 x RER
